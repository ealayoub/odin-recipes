Fettuccine Recipe

Description

This is a pasta made with good ingredients

A gorgeous and delicious piece of cooking mastery

ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

  2. Transfer browned beef into a large pot over medium heat;

    Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and

    garlic salt. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan

    cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.

  3. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
